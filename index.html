About me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Beihang University since 2017. I am advised by Professor Bo Li and affiliated with the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems. My research interests lie within the Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Graph, Knowledge Representation Learning, Knowledge Reasoning and Natural Language Processing. I received my bachelor degree from Beijing Institute and Technology. I have been the Research Intership in DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group and the Youth Innovation Tutor in YouPlan(Beijing).
