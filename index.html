About me

I am an assistant professor in Institute of Artificial Intelligence at Beihang University since 2022. I received Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Bo Li and affiliated with the School of Computer Science and Engineering. My research interests lie within the Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Graph, Knowledge Representation Learning, Knowledge Reasoning and Natural Language Processing. I received my bachelor degree from Beijing Institute and Technology. I have been the Research Intership in DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group and the Youth Innovation Tutor in YouPlan(Beijing).
